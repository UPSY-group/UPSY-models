Description:
This is a low-resolution version of a basic Antarctic initialisation.
It runs for 20,000 years, nudging the basal friction using the H_dHdt_flowline
scheme. Sub-shelf melt rates are also nudged. The resulting ice sheet is
quite stable, and shows a reasonable match to satellite observations.
