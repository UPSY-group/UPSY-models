Description:
The Halfar solution to the SIA gives the ice thickness H(x,y,t),
given a specific initial ice geometry H0(x,y). This is a great
tool for verifying the ice thickness integration module.
The Halfar_static experiment imposes an SMB that is exactly
equal and opposite to the thinning rate dH/dt of the Halfar
solution, so that the ice thickness ideally should not change
at all.
