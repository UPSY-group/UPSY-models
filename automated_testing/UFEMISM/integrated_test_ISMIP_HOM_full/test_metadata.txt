Title: ISMIP-HOM

Keywords:
- idealised geometry
- velocity only
- model intercomparison

URLs:
- https://doi.org/10.5194/tc-2-95-2008

Description:
ISMIP-HOM is a benchmark experiment for higher-order and full-Stokes
models. The idealised geometries are slab-on-a-slope, with periodic
spatial variations in either the bed geometry or the basal friction
coefficient. UFEMISM's Blatter-Pattyn solver generally agrees well
with the ensemble results; the DIVA and particularly the hybrid SIA/SSA
quickly deviate from the ensemble as the wavelength of the
geometry/friction variations decreases.
