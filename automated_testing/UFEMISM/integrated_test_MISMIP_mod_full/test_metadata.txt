Title: MISMIP_mod

Keywords:
- grounding-line migration
- idealised geometry

URLs:
- https://doi.org/10.5194/tc-6-573-2012

Description:
MISMIP_mod is a modified version of the flowline experiment from the first
MISMIP paper (Pattyn et al., 2012). After initialising a steady-state ice
sheet, the uniform flow factor is decreased, causing the grounding line to
advance to a new steady state. Then, the flow factor is reset to its original
value. Ideally, the grounding line should then retreat back to its original
position. Any difference between the initial and final geometry is a numerical
error - which Pattyn et al. showed can be quite substantial. UFEMISM's sub-grid
friction scaling approach works quite well to reduce this error, so that even
with a 10 km resolution, the hysteresis is almost negligible.
